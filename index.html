Let’s meet & build the future of technology

The AKCSSC 2018 conference welcomes all humans in the WORLD to gather and share together their knowledge and experience. The event is about having a day full of inspiring talks and blazing conversations about a future technologies. Join Us on Facebook

Venue

The AKCSSC 2018 takes place at the DB1 Seminar Hall: College of Engineering Trivandrum

How to get there

  • train
  • Bus/Metro
  • Walk

The train station (trivandrum central) is 3min away by foot, just over the kfc.

Alight at the KSRTC Stand or Lulu Mall.

From the train station, cross the road and turn right. The college is 200 meters away.
